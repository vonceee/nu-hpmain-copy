Student interns from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela, Polytechnic University of the Philippines, University of Southeastern Philippines, Bicol University - Polangui Campus, Surigao Del Sur State University, University of Immaculate Conception, Technological Institute of the Philippines, Pangasinan State University and Mariano Marcos State University, are enjoined by HPI President and CEO Engr. Outreach Programs

Our Corporate Social Responsibility aims at contributing to a happy, peaceful, productive and prosperous Filipino society. Industry-academe-community partnership is one commendable way to achieve it.

“The company aims to meet the needs of Academe and Industry, focusing on creating a competitive workforce for a prosperous Filipino society. Outreach to underprivileged members is also vital. During Christmas, Hytec Power Incorporated (HPI) partnered with a rotary club for community service projects. HPI donated an LCD TV to the SPED Department of Commonwealth Elementary School, featured on ABS-CBN's "Mutya Ng Masa."


At the Christmas Party, HPI showcased a robot presentation with NAO, entertaining special children. HPI also provided Fischer Tips educational materials. The Rotary Club offered snacks, while ABS-CBN sponsored meals, toys, and clowns for entertainment.
